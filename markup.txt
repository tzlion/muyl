== MUYL MARKUP == v0.0.3 WIP

Used for user input into text fields

V simple

    ::bold::

    __italic__

    * Bulleted list item
    * Another bulleted list item

    # Numbered list item
    # Another numbered list item

    Internal link: [[type shortname/item id|link text]] << COMING BACK

    External link: [http://example.com Link text] OR just [http://example.com]

    External image: {path/to/img.jpg Alt text} OR just {path/to/img.jpg} OR {path/to/img.jpg 420x69 Alt text} OR {path/to/img.jpg 420x69} (this is new!)

    =L1 header=
    ==L2 header==
    ===L3 header===
    ====L4 header====
    =====L5 header=====
    ======L6 header======

    newline = newline
    2x newline = new paragraph

To just display any special character without its effect, precede it with a backslash
eg \__not italic\__ would output "__not italic__" (and not "not italic" in italics)


anything not mentioned is not supported yet
